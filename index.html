Chicken Fried Rice

  • 20 min
  • Easy
  • 2 servings

A quick and easy fried rice with chicken, vegetables and a delicious savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 200g chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  2. 2Add garlic and onion. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. 3Add chicken and cook until fully cooked.
  4. 4Push everything to one side and scramble the egg.
  5. 5Add the rice and soy sauce. Mix well.
  6. 6Season with salt and pepper and serve hot.
